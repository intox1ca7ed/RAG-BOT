Forwarding services
Our company will take on the following tasks:
•Preparation and verification of all necessary documentation, including filling out the GTD (goods declaration), DTS, and customs clearance of cargo
•Performing loading and unloading operations, engaging talmans, stevedores, adjusters, surveyors, and other specialists whose services may be required
•Transportation and storage of cargo, including the preparation of all necessary documentation, and the search and acquisition of vehicles for intra-port transportation
•Searching for and attracting vehicles (railcars, cars) to remove goods from the port and transport them to their destination
•Providing consulting services for choosing the optimal route outside the port, legal services for customs clearance of goods, etc.

Thanks to well-established connections with the CMA CGM shipping line and
reliable connections with the VMPT , we carry out sea container cargo
transportation from all ports of China to Russia. The company's exports are
carried out through the Vostochny port in Nakhodka.
