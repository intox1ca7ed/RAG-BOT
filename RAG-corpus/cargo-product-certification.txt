Product certification
Product certification may be required for customs clearance of imported goods.
According to the lists established by the state regulatory authorities, goods entering
the territory of the Russian Federation must undergo mandatory or voluntary
certification, which is necessary for the further free sale of products. Our company's
specialists provide assistance in the certification of products, goods, or services in
accordance with the standards applicable in the Russian Federation.

Thanks to a comprehensive approach to the product certification process and wellestablished connections with various testing laboratories and research centers, the
following is provided:
•Obtaining a certificate of conformity
•Obtaining a hygienic certificate (sanitary and epidemiological certificate)
•Conducting voluntary certification
•Getting a declaration of conformity
•Getting a fire certificate
•Certification of products according to State Construction standards
•Obtaining an Import Quarantine Permit (IQP)
•Certification of flour, bakery, and pasta products
