Customs clearance
We provide services:
•preparation of documents for customs declaration processing
•documentation processing, including electronic samples, for customs procedures
•declaration of transported products by customs control authorities
•providing the Russian customs authority with all the necessary documents and information required for customs clearance
•organization of payments related to customs operations and other payments
specified in the Customs Code of the Russian Federation regarding vehicles and goods
•customs clearance services as an authorized person for vehicles and goods
•representing the customer's interests at the customs clearance point for equipment and other cargo
•conducting foreign economic activity consulting
•classification of goods according to the Customs Code of Foreign Economic Activity
•calculation of the cost of customs expenses, as well as information about
customs rates
