Cost of services

Payment methods:
•Cash payment in dollars or yuan — payment is made in China. You can take out of
Russia an amount of up to 10,000 dollars per tourist without a declaration and
bank reference. If the amount exceeds 10,000 dollars, a declaration and bank
reference will be required at customs when leaving the country.
•Payment by bank card - cashless payment in China. You withdraw yuan from the
card at any ATM in China. Visa, Master card are accepted. Be sure to check with
your bank what percentage is withdrawn in ATMs for card maintenance, as well
as whether there are any restrictions on the size of the withdrawn amounts.
Some banks set a limit on cashing out, for example, no more than 40,000 US
dollars per month.

Payments for goods under the agreement

Payment for furniture, finishing materials, decorative items, and other goods
purchased during the Furniture Tour is made in two stages:
1. During the trip, the product is selected and prepayment is made to the suppliers
(from 5 to 30% for custom-made furniture and 100% for available furniture or
special price offers)
2. At the end of the trip, a report on the purchases made is prepared (total
purchase amount, prepayment amount, second payment amount, and
production time). The second payment is made after the report is approved and
includes an additional payment for the product and payment for the services of
Furniture Tour.
